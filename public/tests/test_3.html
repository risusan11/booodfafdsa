Part 3: Reading (10 Questions)

Passage A

Tom plays basketball after school three times a week. He practices with his friends at the gym. He wants to join the school team next year.

16. How often does Tom play basketball?
17. Where does Tom practice?
18. What does Tom want to do next year?
Passage B

Lisa likes cooking. She makes breakfast for her family every weekend. She wants to learn how to make more dishes, so she watches cooking videos online.

19. What does Lisa do every weekend?
20. What does Lisa want to learn?
21. How does Lisa learn cooking?
Passage C

Ken has a part-time job at a supermarket. He works there on Saturdays and Sundays. He likes talking to customers and wants to work there full-time in the future.

22. When does Ken work?
23. What does Ken like about his job?
24. What does Ken want to do in the future?
